Lemon infused falafel

Change it up! infsuse your falafel! In this recipes we infuse raw Chickpeas with Lemon and herbs to bring your falafel experience to a new level

ingredients

  • Uncooked Chickpeas
  • Fresh Lemon, pressed
  • Onions
  • Fresh thyme
  • Fresh Rosmarin
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Some whitewine or champagne
  • Water
  • Salt and pepper

Prep

Combine Chickpeas, the lemonjuice, Onions, thyme, rosmarin and cilantro in a bowl. Add around 200ml whitewine or champagne and fill the bowl with water. let the chickpeas soak for at least 4h or overnight.

Cooking

Rinse the water from the bowl (replace herbs with fresh ones) and put everything into a blender. Blend until fairly smooth. make the chickpeas into balls and deep or shallowfry them for about 5-10min
